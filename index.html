Instructions

Spensor is trapped on an island for the next 27 days.

If his water, food or sanity levels reach 0...he dies!

Each passing day, he looses a water and food level.

Each night Spensor sleeps away from camp, he looses a sanity level.

Spensor is allowed 4 searches per day. The more he searches, the farther he gets away from camp.

Each search can result in finding food, coconuts, nothing, or nasty island trolls!

A coconut can be used to replenish a water level or used to defend against island trolls.

If Spensor has no coconuts when attacked, he starts loosing his sanity.

If Spensor defeats 5 trolls, the Master Troll gets angry and attacks!

5 coconuts are needed to defeat the Master Troll. Spensor can attempt with less, but the chances are slim.
