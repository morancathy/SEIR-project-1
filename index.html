Welcome to

Man on an Island

X

Spensor is trapped on an island for 21 days.

If his water, food or sanity levels reach 0...Spensor dies!

Each passing day, he looses a water and food level.

Daily Searches

Spensor is allowed 4 searches per day.

Each search results in finding food, coconuts, nothing, or nasty island trolls!

The more he searches, the farther he gets away from camp.

If Spensor can't find his way back to camp, he looses a sanity level.

Trolls

Beware of trolls, they can take your sanity!

If Spensor defeats 5 trolls, the Master Troll appears and Spensor has a chance to take his riches!

5 coconuts are needed to defeat the Master Troll. Spensor can attempt with less, but chances are slim.
